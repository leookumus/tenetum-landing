What is Tenetum?

Tenetum is a next-gen SaaS solution designed to address the complex world of digital privacy. We use a microservices approach — including dedicated engines for consent management, cookie scanning, and ML optimization — to deliver real-time compliance without compromising performance.

Our platform enables organizations to automate the entire privacy lifecycle. With a Pay-As-You-Go model that grows with your traffic, we replace complex compliance activities with a transparent, data-driven Governance Center.

Intelligent Consent Engine

Sub-50ms response times, IAB TCF 2.2, Google Consent Mode v2, and region-aware banner delivery.

Deep-Dive Security Scanning

12 analyzers run in parallel — cookies, fingerprinting, dark patterns, CHIPS, and 29 cross-rule validators for correlated violations.

8+ Global Regulations

GDPR, KVKK, CCPA, LGPD, PIPEDA, APPI, PDPA, UK DPA — plus 20 US state privacy laws in one engine.

9 Microservices, One Platform

Consent, Cookie, Scanner, DSR, Geo-IP, ML, Analytics, Integration Hub, and Governance — working as one.
